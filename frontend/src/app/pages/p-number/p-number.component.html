Personal Identity Number

A Swedish personal identity number (Personnummer) is the main identity number used when living in Sweden. It gives access to healthcare, banking, taxation, employment and many government services.

What It Is

Your unique identification number used throughout Sweden by both government authorities and private companies.

Who Can Get It

Usually people moving to Sweden for one year or longer who are registered in the Swedish Population Register.

Where To Apply

Apply through Skatteverket after moving to Sweden and completing identity verification.
